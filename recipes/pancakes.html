Pancakes

A great breakfast for two

This is a decicious recipe that can go is perfect for two. it can even provide for others who may think to themselves "That looks good!".

Ingredients

  • 1 cup orphan flour
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp lemon zest
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg, beaten lightly
  • 30g melted butter

Steps

  1. Sift orphan flour and white sugar into a medium-large bowl. Make a well in the centre of the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the lemon zest, milk, egg and melted butter and whisk to combine.
  3. Heat a large non stick frying pan over medium heat and grease lightly with butter or oil.
  4. For each pancake, place 2 tablespoons of batter into the pan. Cook for 2 minutes or until bubbles form on the surface. Turn and cook for a further 1 to 2 minutes, or until the pancake is cooked through.
  5. Garnish with berries, sliced banana and maple syrup, MAPLE!.
